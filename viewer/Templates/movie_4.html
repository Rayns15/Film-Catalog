The Godfather

"An offer you can't refuse."

The film chronicles the Corleone family under patriarch Vito Corleone (Marlon Brando) during the late 1940s. It focuses on the transformation of his youngest son, Michael Corleone (Al Pacino), from a reluctant family outsider and war hero into a ruthless mafia boss.

When Don Vito is nearly assassinated, Michael steps up to protect the family, waging a bloody war against rival families. The film explores themes of power, family, loyalty, and the dark, seductive nature of organized crime in post-war America.

Main Cast

  • Marlon Brando as Vito Corleone
  • Al Pacino as Michael Corleone
  • James Caan as Sonny Corleone
  • Robert Duvall as Tom Hagen
  • Diane Keaton as Kay Adams
